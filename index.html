About TechXpo 2025

TechXpo 2025 was a prominent technology exhibition and competition organized by the IEEE Computer Society of the University of Kelaniya. This prestigious event brought together innovative minds from across the country to showcase their technological advancements and creative solutions to real-world problems.


The event featured various categories including robotics, IoT solutions, software development, and hardware integration. As a participant, I had the opportunity to demonstrate my skills in electronics and programming through two main projects: an ESP32-based Robotic Car and an ESP32 Weather Sensing Module.

ESP32 Robotic Car

A versatile robotic car built using an ESP32 microcontroller with Wi-Fi capabilities. Controlled via a custom mobile app and equipped with ultrasonic sensors for autonomous navigation.

Key Components:

  • ESP32 Development Board
  • L298N Motor Driver
  • HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor
  • DC Motors with wheels
  • LiPo Battery
View More

ESP32 Weather Sensing Module

An IoT-based weather monitoring system that collects and transmits environmental data to the cloud. Designed for real-time data analysis and remote, energy-efficient operation.

Key Components:

  • ESP32 Development Board
  • DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensor
  • BMP280 Barometric Pressure Sensor
  • UV Light Sensor
  • Solar Panel + Battery Management
